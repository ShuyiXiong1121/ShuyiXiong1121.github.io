About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music.

Shuyi has explored a wide range of genres, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental work, scores, and FX for different contexts.

Shuyi has contributed to numerous projects, including but not limited to music for DESCENTE's Beijing Great Wall runway show, a visual-music project for the China Academy of Art, sound design for a well-known Shanghai musical, and sound design for a comic exhibition.
