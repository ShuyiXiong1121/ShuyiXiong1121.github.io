About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music. His work spans a wide range of styles, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental projects, as well as scores and FX for different contexts.

Shuyi has contributed to a variety of projects, including music for DESCENTE’s Great Wall show in Beijing, visual scoring for China Academy of Art final projects, sound design for a well-known Shanghai musical, and soundscapes for a comic exhibition.
