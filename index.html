About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music.

Shuyi has explored a wide range of styles, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental projects, scores, and FX for different contexts. He has been involved in numerous productions, including music for DESCENTE’s Great Wall runway show in Beijing, visual scoring for final projects at the China Academy of Art, sound design for a well-known Shanghai musical, and audio work for comic exhibitions.
