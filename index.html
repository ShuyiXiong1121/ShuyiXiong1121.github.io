About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music.

Shuyi has explored a wide range of genres, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental projects, scores, and FX for different contexts.

Shuyi has also worked on a variety of external projects, including music for DESCENTE’s fashion show on the Great Wall in Beijing, end-of-term visual scoring projects for the China Academy of Art, sound design for a well-known Shanghai musical, and audio for a comic exhibition.
