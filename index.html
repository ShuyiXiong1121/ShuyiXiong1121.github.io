About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music.

His work spans a wide range of styles, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental projects, as well as scores and FX for different contexts.
