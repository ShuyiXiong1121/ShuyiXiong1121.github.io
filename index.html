About

I am an Electronic Production & Design major at Berklee College of Music. I love creating detailed sound worlds, rhythmic textures, and concept-driven pieces that connect emotion, motion, and space.

Short bio

I work across electronic music production, sound design, and performance. I enjoy building tracks from unusual starting points: a groove, a sound texture, a harmonic idea, or even a visual concept.

  • · Current major: Electronic Production & Design
  • · Main tools: Ableton Live, modular synths, TR-8S, software synths
  • · Interests: UKG, techno, experimental sound, interactive media

Skills & focus

  • · Music production: composing, arranging, mixing electronic tracks
  • · Sound design: textures, atmospheres, FX, synth programming
  • · Performance: live sets, hybrid hardware/software rigs
  • · Technical: MIDI routing, CC automation, basic coding for interaction
