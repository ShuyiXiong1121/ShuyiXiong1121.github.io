About

I am an Electronic Production & Design student and electronic music producer. I enjoy building detailed sound worlds that focus on groove, texture, and small shifts that keep a track alive.

This website is coded with simple HTML and CSS, plus a small amount of JavaScript if needed. The goal is to keep the layout minimal and readable, with a dark blue / violet palette and a hint of red to match my artist identity.
