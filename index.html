About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music.

Shuyi works across a wide range of electronic styles, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental work, scores, FX for different contexts, and more.

Shuyi has contributed music and sound to a variety of projects, including (but not limited to) DESCENTE’s Great Wall show in Beijing, visual scoring work for China Academy of Art projects, sound design for a well-known musical in Shanghai, and audio for comic and exhibition spaces.
