About

I am an Electronic Production & Design student who enjoys building detailed sound worlds and rhythm-driven tracks. I like starting projects from different places: a groove, a sound design idea, a chord progression, or even a visual or story concept.

This site is part of my programming class final project. It is built with basic HTML, CSS, and a small amount of JavaScript. The goal is to practice core web skills while making something I can actually use as an artist.
