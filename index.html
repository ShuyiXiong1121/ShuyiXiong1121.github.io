About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music.

Shuyi has explored many styles, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental work, scores, and FX for different scenarios.

Shuyi has also contributed music and sound to a variety of projects, including (but not limited to) the DESCENTE Great Wall runway show in Beijing, visual scoring for final projects at the China Academy of Art, sound design for a well-known musical theatre production in Shanghai, and audio for comic exhibitions.
