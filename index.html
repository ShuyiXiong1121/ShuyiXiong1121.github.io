About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music. He enjoys building detailed sound worlds that focus on groove, texture, and small shifts that keep a track alive.

His work spans a wide range of styles, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental projects, as well as scores and FX for different contexts. Shuyi has contributed music and sound to projects including the DESCENTE Great Wall show in Beijing, visual scoring pieces at China Academy of Art, sound design for a well-known Shanghai musical, and soundscapes for a comic exhibition.
