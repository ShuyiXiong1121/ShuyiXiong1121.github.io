About

Shuyi Xiong is an electronic producer and multi-instrumentalist from Shanghai, China, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music.

Shuyi has explored a wide range of styles, including techno, UKG, future bass, trap, psy-trance, experimental projects, and soundtrack-style work, as well as sound design and FX for different contexts.

Beyond personal releases, Shuyi has contributed music and sound work to various projects, including music for DESCENTE’s Great Wall runway show in Beijing, score and sound for a China Academy of Art visual project, sound design for a well-known Shanghai musical, and audio for a comic exhibition.
